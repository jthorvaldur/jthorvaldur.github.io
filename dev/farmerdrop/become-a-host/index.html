Host a HostDrop

Offer a convenient pickup spot for your neighborhood. Hosts turn a driveway, porch, or storefront into a weekly community food hub — on a schedule that works for you.

Your side

What you'll do

  • Offer a weekly pickup window at your location
  • Welcome members collecting their orders
  • Coordinate with farmers delivering to you
  • Help grow your neighborhood's food network
Requirements

What you'll need

  • A safe, accessible pickup spot
  • A few hours during each drop window
  • Basic contact and location details
  • An application we review before you host
Logistics

How hosting works

  • Farmers deliver to your HostDrop in bulk
  • Members pick up within the drop window
  • You confirm handoffs — no inventory to manage
  • Delivery areas and farmers are matched over time
Community

Why hosts matter

  • HostDrops are the backbone of the network
  • You strengthen local, direct food access
  • Flexible commitment — host as often as you like
  • Recognition in the HostDrop directory
