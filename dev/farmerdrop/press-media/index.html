Our story

Why FarmerDrop exists

FarmerDrop decentralizes the food supply chain — connecting farmers directly to consumers through neighborhood HostDrops. The result is fresher food, fairer economics, and stronger local communities.

Impact & mission

What we're building

As the network grows, neighborhoods become marketplaces and communities shift from big-chain dependency to sovereign value creators. Our mission is direct food distribution that empowers healthy communities.
