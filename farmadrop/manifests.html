Operational structure

One lineage, six views.

A manifest is not one document shared unchanged. Each participant sees the slice needed to act, while source-line identity and quantity conservation remain global.

Participant projections

What each party owns and sees

The platform view joins the projections; it does not overwrite their evidence.

1

Customer / member

Owns
order, order line, receipt decision
Contributes
quantity ordered, substitution acceptance, quantity accepted or returned
Reads
delivery line, source farmer, refund/remedy status
Decides
accept, reject, return, request remedy

2

Farmer / supplier

Owns
fulfillment line, packing item, source lot
Contributes

Reads

Decides

3

Carrier / dropper

Owns
shipment, handoff evidence
Contributes

Reads

Contributes
