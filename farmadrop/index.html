Open Questions — the whiteboard's ask
  • The platform take: confirm the real number (the 10% vs 0.10% ambiguity blocks the model — pick a template or propose a fourth).
  • Volkmar's repo: what does the test app / ledger / ".7 backend" actually cover vs. what this map assumes?
  • The six ledgers: name them (RQ-1 on The Books page). The whole ledger spec is a partition over accounts — regrouping to the real six is cheap, guessing wrong silently is not. And: how do partial-refund tax reversals reach Stripe Tax today?
  • Credit boost funding: the 2% boost needs a named source — breakage, the 0.25% wedge, or float.
  • Incumbent baseline: sanity-check the two-hop economics against real Alberta / Texas farmgate numbers.
  • Drop Score tree: which indicators are wrong, missing, or ungameable in practice?
  • The church network: which "12,000 churches" is it — Assemblies of God (districts, top-down) or Churches of Christ (autonomous, one-at-a-time)? The go-to-market motion depends on it.
