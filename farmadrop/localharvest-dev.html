Boundary

Experience surfaces are not financial facts

Applications establish candidate identities and preferences. A scan, accepted handoff, Stripe balance transaction, tax event, or balanced ledger event establishes a different kind of fact. The proposed schema keeps those sources separate and links them through durable participant, order-line, manifest, and event identities.
