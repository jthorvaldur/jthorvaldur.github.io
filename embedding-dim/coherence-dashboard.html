Detection Points

The coherence metric detects degradation earlier than variance-based methods. The composite Delta(t) captures cross-component correlations that individual variance monitors miss.

Coherence detection — first threshold crossing of composite Delta
Variance detection — first individual sub-component exceeds 2-sigma
Failure onset — system enters non-recoverable degradation
