Orwell Explorer — Documents in 15-Dimensional Doublespeak Space

Every document is a 15-vector: 7 composite axes (weighted into the original Orwell score) plus 8 diagnostic axes (hedge, temporal vagueness, authority laundering, nominalization, numerical evasion, pronoun rot, adjective inflation, sentence burial). Projections collapse that vector to 2D so you can surf the manifold. Labels are anonymized by cohort + sequence; no document text leaves the page.
