ORDER

This matter coming on to be heard on the Respondent's Motion to Compel, the Court having reviewed the Motion, the Response, and the exhibits attached thereto, and having heard argument of counsel, and the Court being fully advised in the premises:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED:

1. The Motion to Compel is GRANTED IN PART.

2. The Petitioner shall produce the following documents on or before July 19, 2025:
   a. Bank statements for accounts ending 4521, 7733, and 9018 for the period January 1, 2023 through December 31, 2024.
   b. Brokerage statements for the Fidelity account ending 2244 for the same period.
   c. Federal tax returns for tax years 2023 and 2024, with all schedules.

3. The Respondent's request for fees is DENIED without prejudice. The Respondent may renew the request if the Petitioner fails to comply with this Order.

4. Failure to comply with this Order may result in sanctions under Illinois Supreme Court Rule 219, including but not limited to the striking of pleadings, the entry of default, and an award of fees.

ENTERED: June 16, 2025
