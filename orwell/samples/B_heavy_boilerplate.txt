PETITION FOR RULE TO SHOW CAUSE

COMES NOW the Petitioner, by and through undersigned counsel, and respectfully submits this Petition in the above-captioned matter. WHEREFORE, it is hereby alleged that the Respondent has been duly notified of the requirements set forth herein and yet, notwithstanding said notice and the directives previously established, the Respondent has not, to the extent that the same may reasonably be required, provided the documentation referenced above.

While the Petitioner cannot speak to the Respondent's intent, it should be noted that the materials, to the extent that they may be deemed relevant by the Court, were not produced in the manner contemplated by the applicable rules. As the Court is well aware, it is well-settled that the failure to provide such documentation in a timely manner constitutes grounds for further action. The Respondent shall not be permitted to introduce evidence except as set forth above and as has been previously established in the prior orders.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Petitioner respectfully directs the Court to the exhibits attached hereto. The Petitioner is not entitled to assume bad faith, although the egregious and willful pattern of conduct may suggest otherwise. Without waiving any objections, and without admitting any of the matters asserted by the Respondent, the Petitioner reserves all rights as may be appropriate. It is hereby ORDERED that the matter shall be set for hearing at the appropriate time, and that all parties shall be directed to appear forthwith and without undue delay.

Pursuant to the applicable provisions, the undersigned counsel certifies the foregoing. RESPECTFULLY SUBMITTED.
