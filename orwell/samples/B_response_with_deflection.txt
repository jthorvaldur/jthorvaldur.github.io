PETITIONER'S RESPONSE TO MOTION TO COMPEL

The Petitioner, by and through undersigned counsel, hereby files this Response to the Respondent's Motion to Compel, and in support thereof states as follows.

The Respondent's Motion is, with all due respect, without merit. As the Court is well aware, the Petitioner has at all relevant times acted in good faith and has, to the extent that production has been reasonably possible, complied with the applicable rules. It is well-settled that the standard of production does not require a party to produce documents that may not be in their possession, custody, or control at the time of the request. Such matters are, of course, subject to the ordinary course of discovery.

The Respondent's allegations regarding timeliness are misplaced. The Petitioner has, in the ordinary course, taken such steps as may be appropriate, and has caused documents to be gathered and reviewed as may be required. To the extent that any documents have not yet been produced, the Petitioner notes that the same will be produced in due course and without undue delay, subject to applicable privileges and objections. The undersigned counsel further notes that the Respondent's request is, in substantial part, overbroad and not reasonably calculated to lead to the discovery of admissible evidence.

Without waiving any objections, and without admitting any of the matters asserted, the Petitioner respectfully directs the Court to opposing counsel's prior representations and to the matters previously set forth herein. The Petitioner reserves all rights and objections, including but not limited to relevance, materiality, and privilege.

WHEREFORE, the Petitioner respectfully requests that this Court deny the Respondent's Motion in its entirety. RESPECTFULLY SUBMITTED.
