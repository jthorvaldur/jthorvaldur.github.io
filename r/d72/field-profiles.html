The Five Measurement Layers

A field node is a measurable intersection point where multiple signal layers converge. The model measures five layers and computes coherence across them.

N
Numerology
Identity code from birth date and full name. Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, Personality, Birthday Number, Maturity.

Planetary geometry at birth. Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Midheaven, and all major planets positioned on the ecliptic.
M
Moon
Lunar phase, illumination, and sign at birth. Gate points (new, quarter, full) are treated as high-activation moments.
G
Geography
Proximity to ley lines, earth grid points, and astrocartography lines. Distance-decay function with 300km half-life.
T
Timing
Current personal year and month cycles, planetary transits, and lunar activation windows.
Classification

Node States

The Δ.72 threshold determines node classification. When layers agree above 0.72 coherence, the system is in a stable or active state.

חסד
Chesed (lovingkindness, mercy) equals 72 in Hebrew standard gematria: Chet (8) + Samekh (60) + Dalet (4). The coherence threshold is set at this value — a system is coherent when its layers agree above the frequency of mercy.
Power Node Coherence ≥ 0.72 AND Intensity ≥ 0.72. All systems aligned and firing.
Stable Node Coherence ≥ 0.72, Intensity < 0.72. Pattern holds but energy is latent.
Distortion Node Coherence < 0.72, Intensity ≥ 0.72. High energy but misaligned.
Transitional Mixed signals — between states, in flux.
Dormant Node Low activation and strength. Latent potential.
