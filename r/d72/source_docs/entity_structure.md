## Key Rules

- All IP held at Coherence Labs IP LLC — licensed down to OpCos via field-of-use agreements, never owned at OpCo level
- Assignments are scheduled and asset-specific — no overbroad blanket assignments
- Holdco equity is 100% Allison unless she expressly approves additional grants in writing
- Joel may hold advisory/economic position in Holdco only if expressly approved; no blocking rights
- Aniroop's interest is scoped exclusively to RootWell & Star LLC — no cross-entity rights
- Delta 72 LLC must not own Coherence Labs IP LLC or RootWell & Star; tied to RecoverMargins only
- RootWell & Star must remain clean for venture funding — no QE economics, no Delta 72 LLC ownership
- RecoverMargins formation structure (Delta 72 LLC ownership %) must be decided before formation documents are drafted
- Any new entity requires Class A (Allison) approval to be added under the umbrella
