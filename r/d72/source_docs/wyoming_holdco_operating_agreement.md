## ARTICLE 3 — MEMBERSHIP CLASSES

### 3.1 Authorized Classes

The Company shall have two classes of Membership Interests:

(a) **Class A Membership Interests** — held exclusively by Allison Hensgen; and

(b) **Class B Membership Interests** — held by [Joel Full Legal Name].

No other class of Membership Interest is authorized. No new class of Membership Interest shall be created without the express prior written approval of the Class A Member in her individual capacity, which approval may be granted or withheld in her absolute and sole discretion.

### 3.2 Class A Membership Interests

Class A Membership Interests carry the following rights, all of which are non-waivable and non-dilutable without the express written consent of the Class A Member:

(a) **Exclusive Management Authority.** The Class A Member is the sole Manager of the Company with full operational and strategic authority as described in Article 7;

(b) **Exclusive Reserved Decision Authority.** The Class A Member holds absolute and exclusive authority over all Reserved Decisions enumerated in Section 8.1, which authority requires no concurrence, approval, consent, or absence of objection from any other Member;

(c) **Economic Majority.** The Class A Member holds [___]% of the Percentage Interest in the Company, representing the majority economic interest;

(d) **Patent Holder Designation.** Allison Hensgen is designated as the named patent holder and inventor of record for all patents filed by or assigned to the Company, subject to inventorship determinations made on a per-filing basis in accordance with applicable patent law and Section 11.3;

(e) **Anti-Dilution Protection.** The Class A Member's Percentage Interest shall not be diluted below [___]% without her express written consent executed as a stand-alone written instrument separate from any other agreement;

(f) **Conversion Authority.** The Class A Member has sole authority to approve, structure, and execute any conversion of the Company to another entity form, including any conversion to a Delaware C-Corp, on terms that preserve her control position as described in Article 19.

### 3.3 Class B Membership Interests

Class B Membership Interests carry only the following rights, and no others:

(a) **Economic Interest Only.** The Class B Member holds [___]% of the Percentage Interest in the Company and is entitled to receive allocations and distributions in proportion to that interest as provided in Article 6;

(b) **Advisory Consultation.** The Class B Member shall be consulted, in a non-binding advisory capacity only, on matters within his area of expertise, including technical architecture, AI and data infrastructure, and cross-entity strategic matters, as further described in Section 9.1;

(c) **Information Rights.** The Class B Member shall receive annual financial statements and such additional information as required by the Wyoming LLC Act, subject to the confidentiality obligations in Article 20.

### 3.4 Class B Membership Interests — Explicit Exclusions

**The Class B Member expressly holds NO consent, approval, blocking, or veto rights of any kind with respect to any decision, action, or matter affecting the Company.** Without limiting the generality of the foregoing:

(a) The Class B Member has no right to consent to, approve, disapprove, condition, or block any Reserved Decision enumerated in Section 8.1;

(b) The Class B Member has no right to consent to, approve, disapprove, condition, or block any other decision made by the Manager in the ordinary course of business;

(c) The Class B Member has no right to consent to, approve, disapprove, condition, or block any amendment to this Agreement proposed by the Manager, except as expressly required by the Wyoming LLC Act with respect to amendments that would alter the Class B Member's economic interest;

(d) The Class B Member has no right to remove, replace, or restrict the authority of the Manager;

(e) The Class B Member has no right to initiate or require dissolution of the Company;

(f) The Class B Member has no right to approve or block any IP licensing, assignment, transfer, or enforcement action;

(g) The Class B Member has no right to approve or block any admission of new members, issuance of new interests, or conversion of the Company;

(h) The Class B Member has no right to approve or block any debt, financing, or fundraising activity of the Company.

**This Section 3.4 is not a limitation on Class B rights — it is an affirmative statement that such rights were never granted and do not exist.**

## ARTICLE 4 — CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

### 4.1 Initial Contributions

Each Member's initial Capital Contribution is set forth in **Schedule B**. Capital Contributions may consist of cash, IP assets, services, or other property, each as valued and agreed by the Manager at the time of contribution.

### 4.2 Additional Contributions

(a) No Member shall be required to make additional Capital Contributions beyond the amounts set forth in **Schedule B** without such Member's written consent.

(b) The Manager may, in her sole discretion, request additional Capital Contributions from any Member. Any Member's refusal to make an additional Capital Contribution shall not constitute a default under this Agreement but may affect such Member's Percentage Interest as provided in Section 4.3.

### 4.3 Failure to Make Additional Contributions

If the Manager calls for additional Capital Contributions and a Member declines to contribute, the Manager may, in her sole discretion:

(a) Seek contributions from third parties subject to the equity restriction provisions of Article 5;

(b) Adjust Percentage Interests proportionally to reflect actual contributions made; or

(c) Cause the Company to obtain financing in lieu of Member contributions.

### 4.4 No Interest on Contributions

No Member shall be entitled to receive interest on any Capital Contribution.

### 4.5 Return of Contributions

No Member shall have the right to demand or receive the return of a Capital Contribution except upon dissolution of the Company in accordance with Article 17, or as otherwise expressly provided in this Agreement. No Member shall have priority over any other Member with respect to the return of Capital Contributions, except as otherwise provided herein.
