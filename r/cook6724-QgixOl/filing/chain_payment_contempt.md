## Conclusion

Contempt for non-payment cannot be sustained when: (1) Petitioner documents $366,929 in payments, (2) Respondent admits receiving $110,041 into undisclosed accounts, (3) Respondent's sworn FDA omits those accounts, and (4) Respondent refuses to disclose the complete financial picture. The Court should compel disclosure before ruling on contempt.
