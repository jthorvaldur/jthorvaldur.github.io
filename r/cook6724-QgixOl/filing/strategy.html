Their Likely Counter-Moves

Counter 1: "The $110K transfers were voluntary, not ordered"

Their argument: "The agreed order says deposit into the joint account. Joel transferred to other accounts on his own. Those weren't ordered payments."

Why it fails:

  • Transfers came FROM the joint account — the same account the order references
  • Money went TO Heather's accounts. She should account for it.
  • Courts have equitable power. A judge seeing $110K going to the opposing party's accounts won't ignore it because it went to ...9056 instead of ...2151.
  • The key question flips: "Where did the $110,000 go? Show us the accounts."
  • Her own Second Contempt Exhibit C lists a $2,000 transfer on 2025-05-06 to "Checking Account x9056" as a violation — acknowledging the account exists while her affidavit omits it.
