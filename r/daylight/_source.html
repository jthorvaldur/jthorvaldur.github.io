How Daylight works

1

Dump everything in

Upload files from your phone, computer, email, or bank. Any format — PDFs, photos, Word docs, even screenshots.

2

Daylight reads and understands

We read every document, even blurry scans and photos taken at angles. We figure out what kind of document it is, who said what, and what was agreed to.

3

See who owes what

Your obligations are tracked automatically. Who is supposed to pay what, by when, and whether they've done it.

4

Know what's missing

Your documents reference other documents. Daylight finds the gaps — the court orders you don't have yet, the financial records you need to request.

5

Get court-ready filings

If an obligation isn't being met, Daylight can draft a motion for you — formatted for your specific court, with the right citations, ready to file.

What Daylight finds in your documents

⚖

Obligations

Who agreed to pay what, transfer what, or do what — and by when. Extracted directly from court orders and stipulations.

📋

Facts

Every important claim, date, amount, and agreement. Tagged by who said it and how reliable it is.

⚠

Missing Documents

Your files reference documents you haven't uploaded yet. We tell you what's missing and why it matters.

📅

Timeline

Every event, filing, and agreement in chronological order. See your whole case at a glance.

📄

Court-Ready Filings

Formatted for your specific court — right margins, right citations, right language. Ready to file.

🔍

Contradictions

Detects when sworn statements conflict with documentary evidence. Surfaces discrepancies automatically.
