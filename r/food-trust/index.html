Decentralized Commodity Exchange

Honey-backed peer-to-peer exchange for agricultural producers. Sovereign trade without fiat middlemen, built on trust structures and coherence-measured quality.

Key Principles

⚖

Sovereignty

Producers maintain full control over their goods, data, and exchange relationships. No central authority dictates terms.

Four-Phase Plan

Phase 1

Legal Foundation

Establish 98 Trust (foreign trust) + UNA (Unincorporated Nonprofit Association) legal framework. Private association structure with no state filing requirements. Formation of the umbrella trust entity.

Trust structures →
Phase 2

Commodity Standard

Define honey as unit of account with grading system. Establish quality metrics via coherence scoring. Set baseline exchange ratios for agricultural commodities against honey weight.

Honey commodity system →
Phase 3

Exchange Mechanism

Build the digital ledger for tracking exchanges. Sovereign digital twins for producers. Merkle-proof transaction records without the overhead of a full blockchain.

Exchange architecture →
Phase 4

Federation & Scale

Regional UNA nodes federate into a network. Additional commodities join the exchange. Cross-regional trade with coherence-arbitrated exchange rates. Protocol standardization.
